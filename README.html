Game Synopsis: Light

"Light" is an interactive game that engages players in the journey of healing and personal growth by overcoming past traumas through decision-making and problem-solving scenarios. The game uses narrative-driven gameplay to simulate various therapeutic techniques and psychological principles, aiming to provide players with a deeper understanding of trauma recovery processes.

Key Psychological Aspects

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT): The game employs scenarios that reflect CBT techniques, where players identify and challenge negative thoughts to promote healthier thinking patterns. This mirrors real-life CBT practices, which are effective in treating a variety of psychological problems, including depression, anxiety, and PTSD. More about CBT.
  • Exposure Therapy: Players encounter controlled exposure to their fears or traumatic memories within the safety of the game environment. This method is based on exposure therapy principles, where gradual exposure to the source of trauma helps reduce fear responses. Learn about exposure therapy.

  • Resilience Training: Throughout the game, players develop resilience by navigating through challenges and learning coping strategies. This simulates resilience training techniques used in therapy to help individuals recover from and adapt to life challenges and adversities.

Educational Value

The game "Light" not only provides an engaging way to explore complex emotional and psychological issues but also serves as an educational tool that illustrates therapeutic concepts and promotes mental health awareness.
